The Cabot station consists of two Panda arms welded to a table (in addition to
a collection of assorted cameras).

There are three flavors which differ on the nature of the grippers.


- `add_riverway_finray_mimic.dmd.yaml`
  - Includes TRI's bespoke Fin Ray fingers attached to a Panda hand.
- `add_riverway_nominal.dmd.yaml`
  - Includes the stock Panda fingers on the Panda hand.
- `add_riverway_without_grippers.dmd.yml`
  - Includes no grippers at all.
